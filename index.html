About Mealmatics

Mealmatics is an AI-powered recipe creation assistant that helps you generate personalized recipes based on your ingredients and preferences.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a recipe?

Simply enter your available ingredients and dietary preferences, and our AI will generate personalized recipe suggestions for you.

How do recipe credits work?

Each recipe generation uses one credit. You can purchase additional credits through in-app purchases to create more recipes.

Can I save my recipes?

Yes! All your created recipes are automatically saved to your personal collection for easy access.
